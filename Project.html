The purpose
of the app

Provide students or parents interested in sending their children to Aethergarde School to have a simple and understandable overview of the school. You can also see the atmosphere in the classroom as well as the VDO that tells the course, all transmitted through AR.

Using
the app

There are 4 working pages: Menu / Chart of Aethergarde School (School Layout) / Ex.Classroom (Classroom Sample) and VDO Courses offered (Course Video).

The Menu page

has 3 buttons to press

, sseparated into Chart of Aethergarde School (School Layout)
/ Ex.Classroom (Classroom Sample) and VDO Courses offered (course videos).





Chart of Aethergarde School (School Layout)

When looking at the Image Target School Layout, a model of buildings within the school will appear.

And if users want to find out where this building is, they can press the side button.
When the button is pressed For example, press the button for School Building 1,
the other buildings will disappear continuously, just School Building 1, allowing the user to see the location of School Building 1.





Ex.Classroom page (Classroom example)

Technique used to create >>Ground Plane when pointing the camera to the ground.

The classroom will appear. Allows users to see the atmosphere in the classroom realistically.
The door of the room can slide open and close. In addition, the location of this classroom is also determined in real time.
and can also press to refresh the location as well






VDO Courses offered page (Course video)

When viewing Image Target VDO Courses offered will show a video clip explaining all
the courses that will be studied if your child is studying at this school.
*** In every scene, there will be a Home button on the bottom left to go back to the menu.
